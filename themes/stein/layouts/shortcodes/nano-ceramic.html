STEIN Premium Ceramic Paint Treatment

STEIN Premium Ceramic Paint Treatment is a high performance coating solution that offers extreme hardness and can be applied to a various surfaces including paint clear coat, plastic, rubber and glass.

STEIN gives exceptional ceramic-like properties and as such is extremely durable and robust, providing high chemical, UV and heat resistance. It not only increases hardness, it leaves a fantastic deep gloss with remarkable gains in surface thickness.

STEIN is a complete treatment, instead of just a coating on top of the paint surface, it goes deep into the paint and crystallizes within the pores thus changing the molecular structure of the clear coat. This means the protection is long lasting and hydrophobic performance will last for years not months.

Once the application is fully cured, it forms a permanent bond within the paint. No chemical or solvent can remove it unless the paint surface is wet sanded or intensively polished with harsh rubbing compound.

STEIN ULTIMA


Stein Ultima diaplikasikan pada cat, kaca, chrome, velg, plastik dan karet, dan bahkan matte! Melindungi semua permukaan hingga 3 tahun perlindungan yang tahan lama. Stein Ultima memberikan perlindungan terhadap sinar UV yang keras, bahan kimia yang keras, air keras, kotoran burung dan bahkan memberikan ketahanan gores. Stein Ultima tidak hanya melindungi kendaraan Anda. Tapi juga akan memberikan tingkat gloss, slickness, dan sifat hidrofobik yang menakjubkan. Menjaga kendaraan Anda lebih bersih, lebih lama, dan membuatnya lebih mudah dibersihkan. Dapatkan pelindung terbaik di dunia saat ini!
